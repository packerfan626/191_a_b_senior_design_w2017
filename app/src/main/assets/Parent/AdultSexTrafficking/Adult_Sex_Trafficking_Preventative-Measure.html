At the start of the grooming process, young people often exhibit behavior that can alert family,friends, and professionals that they may be at risk of being sexually exploited.

  • Awareness
    • Be aware of your surroundings. Travel in groups. Walk in well-lit areas.
    • Get to know the people around you.
  • Speak Up
    • If you see, hear, or suspect that someone is being abused, report it to authorities.
  • Educate yourself and others
    • Learn more about trafficking
